# Project Name

This is a project about Risk Based Internal Audit, designed to support audit planning, execution, monitoring, and reporting based on organizational risk priorities. The project involves enterprise risk management and internal audit capabilities, aiming for control effectiveness and risk analysis to determine preventive action, it focuses on emphasize mitigation and preemptiveness.

## Features

There are a lot of features on RBIA, such as:

1. Audit Charter

- Audit Charter (consists of Piagam Audit)
- Audit Mandate (mandat fungsi Internal Audit)

2. Risk Profile

- Organization Level Risk Profile (berisi profil risiko organisasi) – lihat file Formulir Excel untuk perancangan aplikasi yg sdh dikirim)
- Department Level Risk Profile (berisi profil risiko unit kerja) – nanti tinggal dibuat sesuai contoh di atas)

3. Priority Risk

- Organization Level Priority Risk (berisi daftar risiko prioritas organisasi) - lihat file Formulir Excel untuk perancangan aplikasi yg sdh dikirim)
- Department Level Priority List (berisi daftar risiko prioritas unit kerja) – nanti tinggal dibuat sesuai contoh di atas)

4. Risk Appetite Statement (berisi pernyataan umum RAS)
5. Risk Mitigation & Control (berisi detail rencana mitigasi dari seluruh risiko moderate, moderate to high & high)

- Organization Level
- Department Level

6. Audit Plan

- Strategic Plan Internal Audit
- Annual Audit Plan
- Audit Activity Plan Form
- Audit Assignment letter Form
- Audit Working Paper Form
- Audit Report Form

7. Data Analytics, berisi hasil analisis terhadap data-data yang diakses oleh aplikasi dari berbagai pusat data internal

- Analysis Report: Data Pattern, Trend, Anomaly, Findings & Follow Up

8. Audit Fieldwork

- Interview (Berisi daftar Interviewee dan Interviewer) - berupa form untuk diupload ke sistem
- Observation (Berisi jadwal observasi) - berupa form untuk diupload ke sistem
- Document Collection (Berisi data-data yang dibutuhkan untuk pelaksanaan audit sesuai objek audit) - berisi form untuk diupload ke system
- Sample Data
- Test Controls (Menguji efisiensi dan efektivitas control yang sudah ada - detail rencana mitigasi)
- Working Papers

9. Reporting

- Audit Findings
- Draft Report
- Final Report (Management & Auditee)
- Consulting Report

10. Monitoring Action Taken Reports (ATRs) - Monitoring implementasi rekomendasi langkah langkah koreksi berdasarkan hasil audit
11. Quality Assurance Review

- Regular Self Assessment (berisi laporan hasil asesmen regular terhadap efisiensi dan efektivitas internal audit sesuai ketentuan, dilakukan oleh pihak internal)
- Self Assessment with Independent Validation (SAIV - berisi laporan hasil asesmen mandiri kesesuaian Internal Audit dengan standar GIAS. Hasil asesmen internal kemudian divalidasi oleh ahli eksternal)
- Quality Assurance Review (QAR - berisi laporan hasil asesmen konsultan profesional terhadap kesesuaian internal audit dengan standar global - IPPF 2027 dan GIAS 2024)

12. Internal Audit Performance

- KPI Achievement
- Work Plan Realization

13. Risk Control Dashboard

- Progress report on implementation of Risk Mitigation
- Control Auto Alert (Pengingat otomatis kepada unit kerja ketika resiko residual masih melampaui target pada Organzation dan Department Level)
- Risk Control Effectiveness

14. Risk Report Update (laporan pembaharuan risiko)

- Risk Report Update (laporan trend dari berbagai resiko untuk level organisasi maupun level unit kerja)
- New Risk Recommendation

15. Predictive Analysis (Analisis prediktif untuk trend resiko ke depan)

16. Dashboard

- Enterprise Risk Overview with Risk Heat Map, Risk Residual & Inherent and Its Visualization
- Audit Planning & Coverage
- Audit Execution Status
- Audit Findings & Issues
- Action Taken Reports
- Alerts & Notifications (also at the header)

## Frameworks For Risk Management System

# Priority Risk List

There are potential risks that needs to be determined from many cases in each departments. Variables includes impact level, possibility level, and that will figure the risk level of each quarters in a year

Example:

# Risk Profile

X Axis stands for impact level ranging between 1 (very low) to 5 (very high), Y Axis means possibility level ranging between 1 (very rare) to 5 (very possible to happen). each part has the borders so it will form the cells. add the blue circle with white number referring to the index in the priority risk list.

# Risk Appetite Statement

1. Risk Ranging from Low to Low to Moderate are acceptable and do not require risk mitigation
2. Risk from Moderate, Moderate to High, and High levels must be addressed by implementing appropriate risk mitigation to reduce the level of risk
